## How to Use This Template

1. **Copy this template** to your project's meeting notes folder at the start of each meeting
2. **Fill in the header** with meeting type, date, time, and facilitator
3. **Track attendees** to maintain participation records
4. **Document discussions** using the Topic sections - add as many as needed
5. **Record all decisions** in the Key Decisions table for traceability
6. **Capture action items** with clear owners and due dates
7. **Note risks and blockers** for visibility and follow-up
8. **Use the Parking Lot** to track out-of-scope items without derailing the meeting
9. **Link to previous meeting notes** if applicable for continuity
10. **Share the notes** with all attendees and stakeholders within 24 hours of the meeting

## Template Variations by Meeting Type

### Sprint Planning Meeting
- Focus on backlog items, capacity, and sprint goals
- Include sprint commitment in Key Decisions
- Action items should include story assignments

### Retrospective Meeting
- Emphasize "What went well", "What could be improved", "Action items"
- Key Decisions should include process improvement agreements
- Track follow-up on previous retrospective action items

### Stakeholder Sync
- Focus on status updates, risks, and requests for decisions
- Include project health indicators
- Action items often include stakeholder approvals or feedback

### Design Review
- Include design artifacts and mockups in Notes & Context
- Key Decisions should capture design choices and trade-offs
- Action items typically include design iterations and validations
